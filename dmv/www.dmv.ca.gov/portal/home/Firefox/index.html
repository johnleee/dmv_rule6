The link you clicked or the web address you typed into your browser did not work for some reason. Here are some possible reasons why:

  1. You clicked on a "broken" link. If you have a free moment, please Email the Webmaster the name of the page and link that brought you here.
  2. You may have typed the page address incorrectly. You might try searching the DMV website using the "Search" at the top page to help track down what you were looking for.

You could also try looking in these areas:

  • If you were looking for the DMV Home Page it is http://www.dmv.ca.gov.

  • For other DMV Online Services try the Online Services page.
  • Recent news and links to archives for Press Releases can be found on the Press Room page.
  • Information for Senior drivers can be found on the Seniors Driver page.
  • Information for Teen drivers can be found on the Teens Driver page.
  • To send comments or suggestions please Email the DMV Webmaster.
